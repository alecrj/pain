                         WHAT HAPPENS NEXT
================================================================================

The system will:
• Process each idea through 5 validation stages
• Kill 85-90% that don't meet criteria
• Find 5-15% that are verified pain points
• Log everything to Google Sheets

Expected results:
• 100 ideas → 5-15 verified → 1-2 worth building
• 500 ideas → 25-75 verified → 5-10 worth building

When you find winners (5/5 score):
1. Read full research in "Verified Pains" sheet
2. Find 20 people in that niche (LinkedIn)
3. Ask: "Do you spend X hours on Y task?"
4. If 50%+ say yes → Build the solution!
